Donating




Donating blood does really help people. Giving blood is one of the most selfless gifts you can give to another person. By giving blood, you are potentially saving lives and helping patients with specific conditions recover and lead a normal life. Did you know that blood cells look round and are red, almost like a tablet. There are also some cells called sickle cells. They are an incomplete form of bloodcells and they are shaped like a sickle (A crescent moon). Our goal is to help as many people as we can and we can make this possible if you help us with donating blood.

We can pay you for blood and we will do a good check. This also involves a free blood check so we can make sure you are also healthy. There are many sugary treats and drinks after you do donate the blood. If you are over 30, and are in need of medical assistance you can talk to one of our bursars and they will schedule dates for you to come here.
